About the Founder

A practical educator helping nursing students prepare with clarity and confidence.

Veronica Adomaa, MSN, APRN, FNP-C created Horizonu Health & Education to give future nurses a simpler and more focused preparation experience.

Book a Consultation View Services

Who We Are

Focused support for students who want less overwhelm and more direction.

Horizonu Health & Education is built around clear instruction, practical frameworks, and organized study support. The goal is to help students prepare in a way that feels calm, purposeful, and effective.

Veronica has supported nursing students through academic instruction, practical mentoring, and exam preparation with an emphasis on safety, prioritization, and critical thinking.

Teaching Approach

Simple explanations, structured thinking, and practical preparation.

  • Break down difficult concepts into manageable learning blocks
  • Use safety and prioritization to guide question analysis
  • Help students study with more focus and less confusion

Mission

Equip future nurses with the knowledge and confidence needed for exam readiness.

The mission is to support students with focused teaching, useful strategy, and a clearer path toward safe and confident exam preparation.

Vision

Build a trusted learning platform for organized and practical nursing preparation.

The vision is to create a dependable academic support environment that helps students feel more prepared for both the NCLEX and professional practice.
