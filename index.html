FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about our NCLEX programs, tutoring, and access.

Is Horizonu fully online?
Yes. All lessons, resources, and practice activities are online, so you can study anywhere and anytime.
What’s included in the Comprehensive NCLEX Bootcamp?
High-yield content review, strategy lessons, prioritization & delegation mastery, case breakdowns, and practice question sessions with clear rationales.
Do you offer one-on-one tutoring?
Yes. One-on-one tutoring includes a personalized study plan, weak-area identification, remediation strategies, and live question walkthroughs.
How do I know which program is best for me?
If you’re unsure, book a free consultation. We’ll ask about your timeline, strengths, and weak areas, then recommend the best plan.
Do you provide practice tests that simulate the real NCLEX?
Yes. Our readiness assessments are designed to mirror NCLEX style, pacing, and decision-making so you can practice under realistic conditions.
Can I study at my own pace?
Yes. You can move through lessons at your pace, and use quizzes and rationales to reinforce learning. Group coaching adds weekly live accountability.
